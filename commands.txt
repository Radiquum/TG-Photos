Add this commands to your bot in @BotFather

search - search media as files /search (day, month, year) (value)
searchlist - search media as list /searchList (day, month, year) (value)
searchdate - search media as list /searchDate (month day year: Feb 10 2020)
upload - upload new media (/upload bulk for bulk uploads)
remove - remove media /remove (postId, fileName) (value)
login - login to your Telegram account to allow database updates
update - re-fetch and update database of posts
